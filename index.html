Learning needs to be fun and interesting for long-term success. Let's study this table below.

Learning Method Efficiency Level Pros Cons
Project-Based Learning High Hands-on experience, practical skills development, motivation. Might lack structured guidance, could be overwhelming for absolute beginners.
Online Courses Medium Comprehensive content, flexibility in learning pace. May lack personalized feedback, may become passive learning if not actively engaged.
Coding Bootcamps High Quick skill acquisition, mentorship, networking opportunities. High cost, time commitment, can be intense for some learners.
Open Source Contributions High Real-world coding experience, exposure to industry standards. Requires prior coding knowledge, may involve complex codebases.
Pair Programming Medium Improved problem-solving, teamwork skills, immediate feedback. Requires a partner, might not be suitable for introverted learners.
University/College Courses High Structured learning, theoretical foundations, academic credentials. Expensive, time-consuming, may not focus solely on practical skills.

We all have different preferences when learning something, it's up to you where you're comfortable and easy.
